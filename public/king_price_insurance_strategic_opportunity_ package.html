Mpact Plastic Containers (MPC), a division of the Mpact Limited Group, is the **leading supplier of plastic containers, crates, and pallets in Southern Africa**. Their expertise is critical to the scale and impact of the Fruitful Crate Dance™ movement.

  • 🏭**Extensive Manufacturing Capacity:** With three state-of-the-art plants in Atlantis (Western Cape), Brits (North West), and Castleview (Gauteng), MPC possesses the capacity to produce the **400,000+ branded crates** required for our nationwide activation.
  • ♻️**20-Year Recycling Commitment:** MPC is deeply committed to the circular economy. They provide a **20-year recycling commitment** for the crates, ensuring their long-term sustainability and minimizing environmental impact through their buy-back and exchange model. This aligns perfectly with King Price's™ ESG goals.
  • 🏅**Quality & Innovation:** MPC is renowned for producing durable, high-quality, and innovative plastic products. Their crates are designed for multi-trip, reusable purposes, forming the largest range of Returnable Transit Containers in South Africa.
  • 🇿🇦**B-BBEE Level 1 Supplier:** As a **B-BBEE Level 1 contributor** (via Mpact Versapak, part of the group), partnering with MPC further enhances King Price's™ B-BBEE scorecard, reinforcing its commitment to local economic empowerment and transformation.
  • 💰**Cost-Effective Scale:** The estimated cost for the branded crates is **R71.50 excl. per crate**. This competitive pricing, combined with MPC's manufacturing prowess and recycling commitment, ensures a cost-effective and sustainable solution for the massive scale of the project.
